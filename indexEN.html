Who we are?

TOPO MINIERA performs cadastre, topography, bathymetry, photogrammetry and laser scanning activities both nationally and internationally. Established in 2009, TOPO MINIERA is today an appreciated surveying and cadastral company. The quality of our activities is given by the use of state-of-the-art technical systems, as well as the high qualification of our staff.


The company provides complete solutions for a wide range of activities such as:

  • Cadastral Works
  • Engineering Surveying Works
  • Bathymetry works
  • Mining Works
  • Environmental Protection Works
  • Orthophotoplanes
  • Digital terrain model construction
  • Potential Services for a Wind Farm Project

Executed Works


Topography and Cadastre:

  • Documents for registration in the land register
  • Situation plans
  • Precision level
  • Check works in progress
  • Digital Terrain Model
  • Contour lines
  • Time tracking of works
  • Volume calculations
  • Transverse and longitudinal profiles
  • Level and facade plans

Bathymetry:

  • Depth studies
  • Determination of submerged landform
  • Volume calculations
  • Monitoring of submerged excavations
  • Cross and longitudinal profiles
  • Checks before and after dredging
  • Determination of the quantities of deposited silt

Aerial measurements - UAV technology:

  • Digital terrain model generation
